About the Team

Itachiyama Institute is a powerful, private high school in Tokyo known for having the strongest boys' volleyball team in the Haikyuu!! series.
They are the reigning national champions, and their team is considered the favorite to win nationals in the story.
Key players on the team include the exceptional hitter Sakusa Kiyoomi and the ace libero Komori Motoya.

My Favorite Team Member

My favorite member is Kiyoomi Sakusa, the team's ace and one of the top three high school aces in the country.
He is a wing spiker and ace for the Itachiyama Institute volleyball team, known for being one of the top three high school aces in Japan.
He is a meticulous and clean-freak character who is also known for his blunt honesty.
His personality stems from his fear of germs and crowds, and his high level of personal hygiene is a key part of his character.
